Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Mathematics & Scientific Computing became independent in 2009 and was earlier part of the Department of Applied Sciences & Humanities established in 1986. With 12 faculty members specializing in diverse fields, the department focuses on quality education and research, supported by modern facilities and a well-equipped Computational Laboratory.

Mission & Cognitive Development

To generate skilled manpower equipped with human values and the ability to address mathematical and scientific computing challenges in science and technology for the welfare of society and national development. The department aims to empower students through quality education, modern infrastructure, and an updated curriculum , while preparing them for emerging technologies and industry needs. It also focuses on promoting ethical values, leadership, managerial skills, and interdisciplinary learning and research in accordance with NEP 2020.
